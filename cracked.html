"Cracked" is an interactive encryption and decryption application designed as a comprehensive year-long project for a Year 12 Software Design and Development class. The project journey was meticulously documented, beginning with a problem statement and evolving through in-depth research, design choices, user interface mockups, and the development of pseudocode for the core logic.

The heart of "Cracked" is its educational exploration of cryptography, showcasing a range of classical ciphers which users can employ to encrypt and decrypt messages.
